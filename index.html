About GATE

The Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) is a national-level exam that tests the understanding of undergraduate subjects in engineering, technology, and more.

Future Opportunities After GATE

  • Admission to M.Tech/M.E programs in IITs, NITs, and IISc.
  • Fellowship programs in premier institutes.
  • Jobs in Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) like ONGC, IOCL, BHEL, etc.
  • Research opportunities in India and abroad.
