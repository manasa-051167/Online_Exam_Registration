GATE 2025 Applications

Everything you need to know about applying for GATE 2025.

Eligibility Criteria

To apply for GATE 2025, you must meet the following eligibility requirements:

  • Must have completed or be in the final year of a Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Science, or a related field.
  • There is no age limit to appear for GATE.
  • Foreign students from specific countries are also eligible.

Required Documents

Applicants need to provide the following documents:

  • A scanned copy of a valid photo ID (Aadhaar, Passport, or Voter ID).
  • Final year or degree certificate for proof of eligibility.
  • Recent passport-size photograph.
  • Category certificate (if applicable).
  • PwD certificate (if applicable).
